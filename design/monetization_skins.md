# Monetization And Skins

Future cosmetic work should avoid changing gameplay stats or competitive clarity.

## Skin Categories

- Unit skins: alternate armor, weapons, faction outfits, silhouettes, and star-level treatments.
- Arena skins: different board frames, tile sets, courtyard props, banners, and background environments.
- UI skins: alternate panel frames, button materials, recruitment card frames, and event log styling.
- Seasonal skins: limited-time visual themes for holidays, campaigns, or historical events.
- Faction skins: themed bundles for Romans, Vikings, Mongols, Slavs, and future factions.

## Guardrails

- Keep tile highlights and targeting readable.
- Preserve faction and role recognition.
- Avoid cosmetics that obscure HP bars, star labels, or board occupancy.
- Keep premium visuals compatible with low-poly performance goals.
