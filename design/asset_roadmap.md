# Asset Roadmap

## Near-Term Passes

- Board visual pass: refine the raised stone arena platform, frame, tile readability, and side markers.
- Integrated bench pass: make the reserve row feel attached to the board while preserving responsive UI behavior.
- Environment props: expand low-poly courtyard props such as walls, crates, banners, torch stands, and market clutter.
- Unit silhouettes: improve role readability for Tank, Fighter, and Ranged units with simple equipment shapes.
- UI style pass: continue unifying buttons, panels, cards, labels, and feedback surfaces around the war market theme.
- Item icons: create small readable icons for Training Sword, Wooden Shield, Longbow, War Drum, and future items.

## Later Passes

- Faction-specific unit variants.
- Arena skin blockouts.
- Combat VFX polish.
- Audio SFX pass.
- Mobile readability pass.
