# Art Direction

War Market uses a stylized low-poly look: readable silhouettes, simple shapes, clear colors, and restrained detail that supports fast tactical decisions.

## Visual Pillars

- Castle courtyard and war market arena: the board should feel like a raised tactical platform inside a fortified trading yard.
- Stone, wood, and brass: stone slabs and walls establish weight, wood supports market/reserve elements, and brass/gold accents guide attention.
- Readable from gameplay camera: units, tiles, highlights, and UI must stay legible before decorative detail.
- Skin-friendly system: units, arenas, UI frames, banners, and faction accents should be built so future cosmetics can swap visuals without changing gameplay rules.

## Current Direction

- Board: stylized stone tactical arena with a raised frame and subtle player/opponent half markers.
- Bench: reserve/staging strip visually tied to the front edge of the board.
- UI: dark stone panels, brass accents, light text, compact recruitment cards.
- Units: simple capsule bodies with primitive role/faction attachments.
